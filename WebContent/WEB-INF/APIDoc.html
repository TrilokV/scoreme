• This returns the search query input.
• Returns the details of a particular team, with its stats
• Searches the away team database.
• Consumes: Away Team name that you want to search.
• Example: http://boiling-badlands-7807.herokuapp.com/rest/teams/awayteam/JPL

@Path("updateaway/{awayTeamName}/{awaycommand}")
• Used to update the score of away team
• @Pathparam awayteam and awaycommand is passed to the method in updatescore package
• Consumes: Away Team name that you want to update/Runs1 or Runs2 or Runs3....
• Example: http://boiling-badlands-7807.herokuapp.com/rest/teams/updateaway/JPL/Runs1

@Path("updatehome/{homeTeamName}/{homecommand}")
• Used to update the score of away team
• @Pathparam hometeam and homecommand is passed to the method in updatescore package.
• Consumes: Home Team name that you want to update/Runs1 or Runs2 or Runs3....
• Example: http://boiling-badlands-7807.herokuapp.com/rest/teams/updatehome/CCS/Runs1
